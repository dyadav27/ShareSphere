Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start borrowing items?
Sign up for a free account, verify your identity, and start browsing items. Once you find something you like, send a request to the owner.
What are the deposit and payment methods?
Payments are handled via Stripe or PayPal. A refundable deposit is held during the rental and returned upon successful return of the item.
How do I list my items for rent?
Go to your profile, click “List Item”, and fill out the form with item details, availability, and pricing. Add photos for better visibility.
What happens if an item is damaged?
Our platform covers basic insurance for certain items. Report any damage immediately—owners may retain the deposit, or we’ll mediate a fair resolution.
How do I edit my profile or account settings?
Log in, navigate to your profile, and click the “Edit Profile” button. You can update your contact info, bio, and payment methods from there.
What if there's a conflict with a borrower or lender?
Use the in-app dispute resolution system. Our support team typically responds within 24 hours to help mediate and resolve issues fairly.
Is it safe to rent items from strangers?
Yes! All users are identity-verified, and we offer secure payment handling, deposits, and item tracking to keep the process safe and transparent.
How do returns and late fees work?
Items must be returned by the agreed time. Late returns may incur additional charges. Communication is key—contact the owner if you're running late.
How can I track my transactions?
Go to your dashboard and view your rental history, current items, and payment records under the “Transactions” tab.
Can I cancel a rental before it starts?
Yes, you can cancel up to 24 hours before the rental begins for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee.
